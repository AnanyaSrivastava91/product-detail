Porche 911 GT3 RS...

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is the epitome of track-ready performance, featuring a 4.0-liter flat-six engine that delivers an impressive 520 horsepower. This high-performance sports car is designed with advanced aerodynamics, including a large rear wing and extensive use of lightweight materials like carbon fiber. Its motorsport-inspired suspension and precise handling make it a formidable presence on both road and track. Inside, the GT3 RS boasts a race-inspired cockpit with carbon fiber accents, bucket seats, and a roll cage, ensuring both safety and style. Ideal for driving enthusiasts, it offers an exhilarating and uncompromising driving experience. Additionally, the GT3 RS features a lightweight titanium exhaust system, enhancing its performance and delivering a thrilling exhaust note.

Porche 911 GT3RS is pinacle of engineering and passion.

Engine Specification

Housing an Flat 6 twin turbo charged engine, Porche's are one the only manufacturers to this Engine. The Engine in the TRACK spec 911 GT3 RS has 525ps and 465 Nm torque, enough to propel it to 100 kmph from standing in 3.2secs
